Step-by-step

1. Half Adder

Adds two single bits. Outputs Sum (S) = A ⊕ B and Carry (C) = A · B

2. Full Adder

Takes A, B and Carry-in. Sum S = A ⊕ B ⊕ Cᵢₙ. Carry-out: Cₒᵤₜ = (A·B) + (Cᵢₙ·(A ⊕ B))

3. 4-Bit Adder (Ripple Carry)

Four full adders cascade. Carry ripples from LSB to MSB; final carry C₄ indicates overflow.
